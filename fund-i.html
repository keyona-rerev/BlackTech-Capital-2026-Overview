Original Vision

How our initial fund design informed today's Catalyst Fund

Fund I was designed as a $15M institutional fund with a 4-year deployment period, follow-on reserves, and a comprehensive structure for scaling our investment thesis. This wasn't just a fund—it was a blueprint for how to bring institutional capital to underrepresented ClimateTech founders at scale.

From Blueprint to Build: Understanding the Pivot

The Catalyst Fund isn't a "smaller fund"—it's the tactical execution of the Fund I thesis, adapted for current market realities to build a track record faster. We're not scaling down; we're strategically pivoting to prove the model before scaling up.

While market conditions led us to pivot to the Catalyst Fund, the core elements of Fund I remain foundational to our strategy:

  • Same Thesis: Underrepresented founders in ClimateTech/CleanTech solving real problems
  • Same Team: Identical leadership and committee structure (with strategic additions like Allison Gibson)
  • Same Values: Impact-focused, transparent, founder-friendly approach
  • Adapted Execution: Agile deployment model scaled for current market realities

Key Learnings

How Fund I informed our Catalyst Fund strategy

Market Timing

Large institutional funds face longer fundraising cycles in current market conditions

Deployment Speed

Focused 1-year deployment allows for quicker proof of concept

Cost Structure

Smaller funds require leaner operational models

Partner Alignment

Strategic partnerships (like Equivesto) enable legal fund publicizing

Why the Pivot? The Path Forward

The Catalyst Fund represents our adaptive execution of the same core thesis. This isn't about abandoning the institutional vision—it's about building the proof points needed to make that vision a reality. We're pivoting to prove, not scaling down.

By starting with a focused, deployable vehicle, we can:

  • Demonstrate our investment thesis with an actual portfolio of real companies achieving real outcomes
  • Build a track record with current market dynamics, proving the model works in today's environment
  • Maintain momentum with a pipeline of exceptional founders who can't wait for a larger fund to close
  • Position for future fund scaling with proven results and investor confidence
  • Deploy capital efficiently with a lean operational model that maximizes value to founders

"Fund I remains our institutional blueprint. The Catalyst Fund is how we get there—one focused deployment, one proven investment, one successful exit at a time. This is strategic execution, not retreat."
