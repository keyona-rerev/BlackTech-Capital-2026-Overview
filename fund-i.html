Original Vision

How our initial fund design informed today's Catalyst Fund

Fund I was designed as a $15M institutional fund with a 4-year deployment period, follow-on reserves, and a comprehensive structure for scaling our investment thesis.

While market conditions led us to pivot to the Catalyst Fund, the core elements of Fund I remain foundational to our strategy:

  • Same Thesis: Underrepresented founders in ClimateTech/CleanTech
  • Same Team: Identical leadership and committee structure
  • Same Values: Impact-focused, transparent, founder-friendly
  • Adapted Execution: Scaled for current market realities

Key Learnings

How Fund I informed our Catalyst Fund strategy

Market Timing

Large institutional funds face longer fundraising cycles in current market conditions

Deployment Speed

Focused 1-year deployment allows for quicker proof of concept

Cost Structure

Smaller funds require leaner operational models

Partner Alignment

Strategic partnerships (like Equivesto) enable legal fund publicizing

The Path Forward

The Catalyst Fund represents our adaptive execution of the same core thesis. By starting with a focused, deployable fund, we can:

  • Demonstrate investment thesis with actual portfolio
  • Build track record with current market dynamics
  • Maintain momentum with our pipeline companies
  • Position for future fund scaling with proven results
