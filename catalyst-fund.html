Strategic Innovation: The Equivesto Partnership

A structural advantage that changes the game for smaller funds

Our partnership with Equivesto, an OSC-licensed Exempt Market Dealer, isn't just an administrative detail‚Äîit's a core structural advantage that fundamentally changes how we operate and creates value for both founders and investors.

Legal Public Fundraising

As an OSC-licensed administrator, Equivesto enables us to legally publicize the Catalyst Fund, broadening our investor reach while maintaining full regulatory compliance.

Lower Setup Costs

Setup capped at $25K + 2.5% of funds raised (vs. $100K for traditional funds), meaning more capital deployed directly to founders.

3rd-Party Escrow

All investor funds are held in third-party escrow, providing additional security and transparency for limited partners.

Democratized Access

$10K LP minimum (vs. $100K in Fund I) opens the door to a broader base of impact-focused investors who believe in our mission.

Higher Deployable Capital

At the $500K-$2M level, our leaner cost structure means a higher percentage of capital goes directly to portfolio companies.

Operational Efficiency

Streamlined fund administration allows us to focus on what matters: finding and supporting exceptional founders.
